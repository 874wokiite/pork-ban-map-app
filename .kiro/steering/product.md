# Product Overview

## Product Description
神戸豚饅マップ (Kobe Butaman/Pork Bun Map) - An interactive web application that displays participating stores in the 神戸豚饅サミット (Kobe Pork Bun Summit). The application provides an engaging way for users to discover, locate, and learn about traditional pork bun shops across Kobe.

## Core Features
- **Interactive Store Map**: Google Maps integration showing precise store locations with custom butaman (pork bun) markers
- **Store Detail Modal**: Comprehensive store information including business hours, pricing, features, and high-quality images
- **Store Discovery**: Browse participating stores with detailed descriptions, specialties, and unique characteristics
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first design optimized for various screen sizes and devices
- **Store Image Gallery**: Visual showcase of each store's exterior and signature butaman varieties
- **Contact Information**: Direct access to phone numbers and Google Maps directions

## Target Use Case
- **Food Enthusiasts**: Discovering authentic butaman shops and their unique specialties
- **Tourists & Locals**: Finding nearby participating stores with detailed location information
- **Cultural Exploration**: Learning about traditional Kobe food culture and butaman history
- **Event Participation**: Supporting the Kobe Butaman Summit by visiting participating establishments
- **Mobile Navigation**: On-the-go store discovery and navigation assistance

## Key Value Proposition
- **Cultural Heritage**: Preserves and promotes Kobe's traditional butaman culture, featuring historic shops like 老祥記 (established 1915)
- **Comprehensive Information**: Detailed store profiles including specialties, pricing (¥100-120), and operating hours
- **Visual Experience**: High-quality imagery showcasing both store exteriors and signature butaman varieties
- **Accessibility**: Static site generation ensures fast loading and offline capabilities
- **Local Community**: Supports local businesses by increasing visibility and foot traffic

## Business Context
This application serves as a digital guide for the 神戸豚饅サミット, promoting local businesses and cultural heritage while providing practical utility for discovering authentic butaman experiences in Kobe. The app balances cultural education with practical navigation needs.
